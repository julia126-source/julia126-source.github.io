CHO CHO SAN

Cho Cho San is Japanese food with a twist

This restaurent offers tasty Japanese food with a special dining experience. The chef cooks your food right in front of you while your party is seated around a stove! This is a super fun place that also happens to have great tasting food and should certainly be on the top of your list of places to try.
