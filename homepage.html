GOOD EATS IN THOUSAND OAKS, CALIFORNIA

Some of my favorite places to eat from my hometown of Thousand Oaks/the surrounding areas

If you happen to find yourself in the beautiful suburbs of Thousand Oaks, California, you will probably be wanting some good food.

From being a native of this lovely place, I have acquired quite an extensive list of tasty restaurants to try. You would assume that a small town like this one would not have a wide variety of cuisines, but you will be surprised to learn that Thousand Oaks has everything from Chinese to Italian to Thai.

This website will offer my personal reviews of three of my favorite restaurants:

  • Maestros Steakhouse
  • Cava
  • Cho Cho San
