CAVA

Cava has the best Mediterranean food

If you enjoy Mediterranean food, like falafels, then you will love this place. Cava is set up Chipotle-style where you build you own bowl. You can pick and choose from a wide selection of toppings and finish it off with a dressing. This place is definitely one to visit if you ever find yourself in the Thousand Oaks area.
