MAESTROS STEAKHOUSE

Why this is the best place for steaks in all of Thousand Oaks

Maestros is the place to get steaks, filets, and fish. They cook their steaks in a butter sauce and top with a beautiful display of garnish and sides. Also, the desserts are to die for. If you go you must get the butter cake! If you would like to take a look at the menu you can click here.
